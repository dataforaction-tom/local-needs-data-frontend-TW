Welcome to the NPC local Needs Databank metadata tool

Here you will be able to check and add metadata to your csv in line with the Local Needs Database data standard. We hope this tool will allow you to easily upload your data easily and tell us what your data refers to.

Important - The dataset itself MUST be published in a tabular data format, such as an Excel spreadsheet or CSV file.

  1. Drag a csv onto the purple section below, or click the purple box to select the csv you wish to upload
  2. Add a description of your data describing what this data relates to
  3. Tell us who the publisher of this data is - this would likely be your organisation
  4. You will then asked to tag columns as “Property” or “Value” (columns can also be ignored at this stage if they are not to be included).
  5. You wil then be asked to provide metadata about the columns or the whole sheet - for example "Foodbank users"
